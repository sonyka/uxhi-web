Mahalo to our sponsors

These organizations help UXperience Aloha come alive

This year, our theme, "UXperience Aloha," celebrates the spirit of Aloha and its impact on Hawaii and the design world. Aloha is more than a word it's a philosophy of love, compassion, and community. By embracing these values in our design practices, we can create experiences that are more meaningful, inclusive, and sustainable. Let's bring the principles of Aloha into our work:

Akahai, meaning kindness to be expressed with tenderness;

Lkahi, meaning unity, to be expressed with harmony;

Oluolu, meaning agreeable, to be expressed with pleasantness;

Haahaa, meaning humility, to be expressed with modesty;

Ahonui, meaning patience, to be expressed with perseverance.

Common inquires and FAQs

Who should attend the conference?

This event is ideal for anyone interested in design and innovation, including students, beginners, and senior professionals in UX/UI, graphic, and product design, as well as UX teams, developers, project managers, and creative directors.

Is the conference in-person, virtual, or hybrid?

The conference will be in person only, with options to purchase pre-recorded talks.

What does the registration ticket include?

The registration fee includes a parking pass (if indicated in your registration), swags, entry to all sessions and networking, all-day coffee, breakfast refreshments, lunch, and snacks.

Are there group discounts available?

Currently, we don't offer group discounts. We highly encourage your team to take advantage of the Early Bird tickets, which are already available at a reduced rate.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all sessions will be recorded. Attendees who have purchased the Recordings-Only ticket or the Video Recordings add-on for in-person attendees will have lifetime access to these recordings. Grab your recordings ticket here.

Can I get a refund if I'm not able to attend?

Yes, cancellations made by Saturday, October 19 will receive a full refund. After that date, no refunds can be made.

What are the parking options available for attendees?

Parking passes are included if indicated within your registration and must be picked up during check in and placed visibly on your car dash. Attendees may park in the stalls striped with white paint at the entrance of Lot C, see the parking map. Limited metered street parking is also available.

Who can I contact for more information?

For general inquiries, please email aloha@uxhiconference.com. We also have contact emails for speakers@uxhiconference.com, sponsors@uxhiconference.com, and volunteers@uxhiconference.com

About UXHI

We are a nonprofit organization based in Hawai'i that is dedicated to connecting and elevating the field of human-centered design for our local community.

Our mission is to foster collaboration and innovation in design, technology, and community-driven solutions in Hawai'i and empower UX designers with skills and tools they need to succeed.

Get Your Portfolio Reviewed!

Are you seeking feedback on some of your design work? If youre looking to get your portfolio reviewed at this years conference, heres what you can expect:

  • 30-minutes of in-person one-on-one time with an experienced designer, who will provide you with feedback and guidance on your work

  • After youve signed up for the conference, keep a look out for an email that will provide you with a link to view more info about our reviewers as well as the opportunity to sign up for a slot ahead of time with the reviewer of your choice. Signing up ahead of time also allows you to share portfolio links or specific questions with your reviewer before the conference date, so that you can greater utilize your in-person one-on-one session with them. Sign-ups will be first come, first serve.

  • You will need to bring your portfolio to the conference the day of.

  • In case you didnt get to sign up ahead of time, we will be accepting walk-ins to fill in any empty time slots

  • Please note: Portfolio reviews are only available to in-person conference attendees

If you have at least a few years of experience of working in the design field and you love to help others improve, please consider signing up to be a reviewer!

Volunteer to Review Portfolios.

We are seeking insightful and enthusiastic volunteers to review portfolios for our community at this years conference.

Contribute to the growth and development of up-and-coming designers, get inspired by the new work youll see, and practice your feedback-giving skills!

Portfolio review volunteers will:

  • Be available on Saturday, October 26th for at least one 1-hour time slot, in-person at the Entrepreneurs Sandbox (scheduling to be coordinated at a later time)

  • Spend 30-minutes one-on-one with somebody giving thoughtful, actionable feedback on their provided work (2 one-on-one sessions in the 1-hour time slot)

  • Get a discount on the ticket to the conference!

If you have at least a few years of experience of working in the design field and you love to help others improve, please consider signing up to be a reviewer!
